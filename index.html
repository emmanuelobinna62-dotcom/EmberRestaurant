About Us

A Passion for
Exceptional Dining

Nestled in the heart of downtown, The Ember Table has been a beloved culinary destination since 2015. Our philosophy is simple: source the finest local ingredients and transform them into unforgettable dishes that honor tradition while embracing innovation.

Chef Marcus Chen brings over two decades of experience from renowned kitchens across the country. His commitment to seasonal menus and sustainable practices ensures every visit offers something new to discover.
